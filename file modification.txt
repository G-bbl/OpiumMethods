## How to remove Elevation Request (Administrator stuff) ⚠️ Do not use on cheats that arent fully external
https://loves-ferrets.lol/1amdipcc.mp4

**Stuff to paste into resource hacker to remove elevation:**

```<?xml version='1.0' encoding='UTF-8' standalone='yes'?>
<assembly xmlns='urn:schemas-microsoft-com:asm.v1' manifestVersion='1.0'>
  <trustInfo xmlns="urn:schemas-microsoft-com:asm.v3">
    <security>
      <requestedPrivileges>
        <requestedExecutionLevel level='asInvoker' uiAccess='false' />
      </requestedPrivileges>
    </security>
  </trustInfo>
</assembly>```
